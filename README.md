## 🧑‍💻 About Me

**Kriengkrai Phongkitkarun (Heng)** — Applied AI Engineer & Technical Lead based in Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand.

I architect AI-assisted software applications, orchestrate multi-omics data pipelines, and drive digital transformation. My expertise bridges the gap between complex exploratory data (spatial transcriptomics, whole-slide pathology, retail databases) and practical, reproducible software systems.

- 🔬 Currently a **Bioinformatician** at Faculty of Medicine, Siriraj Hospital, Mahidol University
- 🚀 Co-founder & CTO at **Happlen** — an early-stage AI startup building personalized health agents
- 🎓 M.Eng. in **Biomedical Engineering** | B.Eng. in **Chemical Engineering** — Mahidol University
- 🏆 **Super AI Engineer Season 6** participant (Batch 6, ID: 602104)
- ☁️ **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner**
- 🌱 Always learning — currently deep into LLM orchestration, RAG systems, and Vision Foundation Models

## 🔬 Selected Projects

| Project | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🏥 **Morpho-Metabolic Foundation Models** | Leveraging pathology foundation models (Virchow2, UNI2-h) on H&E whole-slide images to predict metabolic scores across massive cohorts (TCGA, n=516) |
| 🤖 **Happlen — AI Health Agent** | iOS-first personalized AI agent with retrieval-based memory, OCR review pipelines, and human-in-the-loop AI workflows |
| 🏪 **Sorkorattool Digital Transformation** | Migrating 373,000+ sales records and 27,000+ products from paper to structured databases with AI-assisted categorization |
| 🧬 **Sarcoma Spatial Transcriptomics** | Analyzed CosMx 6K spatial data of ~1.94M QC-passing cells across 15 sarcoma lesions using K-means, UMAP & Sankey visualizations |
